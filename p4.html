Artist Statement

The work uses one breadboard, Raspberry Pi Pico W, A cardboard box and some other components connected to the breadboard. The components are used with the pigtail wires connections are 3 digital outputs using 3 LEDs, 2 digital inputs 1 momentary button, and 1 toggle switch, 1 analog output as the third led, 2 analog inputs using 1 pot and 1 photocell, and one digital output. The songs that are played are two haunted mansion themes. The is the toggle for turning off the son. The Monmentary button changes the song and photocell changes effects. The potentiometer changes the volume of the music.

The way the wires are slightly short for the box was a challage and the way the hardware works once it's pluged in correct was straght forward.
