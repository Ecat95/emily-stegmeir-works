Artist Statement

The work uses one breadboard, Raspberry Pi Pico W, a toggle switch, a button, an amp, a speaker, three LEDs that use neopixels, a GSR sensor, and The Oximeter. The images are modified in the Processing project. The GSR sensor, led neopixels, and oximeter were added to the microsyth work. The software was challenging to get the processing part working. The videos show the working leds in the first with the software. Then how the hardware works and the processing part of the work.
